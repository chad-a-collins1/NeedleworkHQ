Business Cards

 Business Cards can be a hastle to keep up with and quite often get misplaced or lost. ReminderMaster2.2 makes it easy to store an unlimited number of business cards. Just enter the data you need from a card that someone gave you into ReminderMaster2.2 and don't worry about it again. Whenever you need it, you know exactly where it is.
