Intuitive, Easy to Use Interface

 The interface is simple and intuitive. No instructions are necessary, just click any of the blue menu buttons and you will know what to do. Despite the easy-to-use interface, ReminderMaster2.2 is very secure. After 65 seconds of inactivity, the ReminderMaster2.2 locks so you will have to re-login. This is to prevent someone from snooping incase you leave your PC or workstation. The data in the database on your PC is encrypted. Only the ReminderMaster2.2 can read your data. This means that ONLY YOU can read your data!
