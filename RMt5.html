Email Addresses

 Store an unlimited amount of email addresses!  Just like with phone numbers, it is convenient to have a list of email addresses that can be quickly accessed by name or quickly edited or added when you receive an email address from a means other than via email (e.g. business card, via phone or conversation, loose paper, etc.)
